About Me

HEECHUL JUNG received the B.S. degree from Sejong University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2007, the M.S. degree from the Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), Gwangju, South Korea, in 2010, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), in 2018, under the advisement of Professor Junmo Kim. He is currently an Associate Professor at the Department of Artificial Intelligence, Kyungpook National University, South Korea. His research interests include optimization, deep learning, and computer vision.
